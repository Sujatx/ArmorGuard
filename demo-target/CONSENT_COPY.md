# ArmorGuard Consent Acknowledgment

Before initiating an ArmorGuard security scan, the operator must explicitly acknowledge the following:

> **Operator Consent & Authorization**
> 
> "I confirm I am authorized to test this target. I understand this scan may include active exploitation attempts depending on the scan mode selected and that my IP address and acknowledgment will be logged for audit purposes. I assume full responsibility for any service disruption or data access that may occur during testing."
